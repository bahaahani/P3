About DANAT

Our History

DANAT was founded in 1995 by a group of passionate gemologists who shared a vision to promote the understanding and appreciation of gemstones in the Middle East. Since its inception, DANAT has grown to become a leading authority in the region, providing education, research, and services related to gemology.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to advance the knowledge and appreciation of gemstones through education, research, and community engagement. Our vision is to be the premier gemological institution in the Middle East, recognized for our expertise, integrity, and commitment to excellence.
