History of DANAT

DANAT was established in 2017 as a initiative to protect and enhance Bahrain's pearling legacy. Our institute has quickly become a global leader in gemological research and education, with a special focus on pearl testing and authentication.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To advance gemological knowledge through cutting-edge research and world-class education, while preserving Bahrain's rich pearling heritage.

Our Vision

To be the world's foremost authority on pearl and gemstone research, education, and authentication.
