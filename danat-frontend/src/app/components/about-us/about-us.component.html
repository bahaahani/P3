Our Mission

At DANAT, we are committed to advancing the field of gemology through cutting-edge research, education, and authentication services. Our mission is to establish Bahrain as a global center of excellence in gemstone and pearl testing, while preserving the rich cultural heritage of the pearl industry in the region.
